What Is PetFolio ?

What Makes PetFolio Different From Other Websites ?

Before starting this project, our team carefully reviewed many existing websites. What we noticed was clear: most platforms focus on veterinarians, but very few truly support pet owners.

There was no single place where pet owners could add their pets, track vaccinations, manage monthly care routines, store health information, or save important notes such as allergies or past surgeries all in one system.

With PetFolio, this is possible.

You can find the nearest veterinarian and save your appointments using our calendar feature. This way, you never forget an appointment. Your scheduled visits are also sent to your email as notifications, helping you keep a clear record of when each care or treatment was done.

PetFolio becomes a personal agenda for your pet‚Äôs health, so you can always feel organized and at ease.

Is PetFolio Safe ?

Veterinarians can view the information you enter through their own PetFolio accounts. This allows them to provide better care and treatment for your pet, as they have all the necessary information available.

According to our privacy policy, we do not have the right to view any personal information except your login details. When veterinarians access your pet‚Äôs profile, they can only see the information written on that specific pet profile.

While helping you make the best decisions for your pet, we also place great importance on your security and privacy. Your trust means everything to us. It allows us to improve our platform, grow stronger, and bring PetFolio to pet owners all around the world.
